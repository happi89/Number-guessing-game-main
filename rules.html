Number Guessing Game

How to play:

  1. Guess a number between 1 and 50 (normal difficulty) by entering it in the input field.
  2. Check if your guess is correct by clicking the "check" button or by pressing the enter key.
  3. If your guess is incorrect the game will inform you if the guess was too high or too low.
  4. If your guess is close by 5 numbers the game will inform you if the SECRET NUMBER is a bit higher or a bit lower
  5. Continue guessing until you get the correct number.
  6. Once you have guessed the correct number, the game will inform you that you have won and display your score in terms of the number of attempts it took you to guess the number.
  7. If you run out of tries the game will inform you that you have lost
  8. The game will also keep track of your highscore(lowest number of tries), and display it and your username.
  9. You can play again by clicking the "Try again" button, and a new number will be generated.

Difficulty Levels:

  • Select the difficulty level you want to play on (easy, normal, hard).
  • Easy: The player gets 15 tries to guess the number and the range is between 1 to 20
  • Normal: The player gets 10 tries to guess the number and the range is between 1 to 100
  • Hard: The player gets 5 tries to guess the number and the range is between 1 to 100

ETC

  • You can press the question mark to reveal the number early
  • click username to change username
